## Notification Type Guidelines

If notification_type is:
- "approval_notification": Congratulatory tone, include authorization number, effective dates, any conditions
- "denial_notification": Empathetic but factual, include appeal rights and deadlines, avoid clinical jargon
- "information_request": Urgent but professional, specify exact documents needed, include submission deadline
- "status_update": Informational, include current status and expected next steps
- "appeal_acknowledgment": Confirm receipt, include timeline expectations
